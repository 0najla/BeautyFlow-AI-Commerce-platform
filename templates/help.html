Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

BeautyFlow is an AI-powered collaborative import platform designed specifically for beauty businesses in Saudi Arabia. We help small businesses create custom product packaging, access AI-generated recommendations, and share shipping costs through our collaborative import system

Our AI Custom Packaging feature uses advanced AI (DALL-E) to generate unique packaging designs based on your preferences. Simply answer a few questions about your product type, style preferences, and describe your vision - our AI will create professional packaging designs for you!

Collaborative shipping allows multiple businesses from the same region to share shipping containers, significantly reducing import costs. Groups of 5 users can join together, and once the group is complete, shipping begins within one week.

Yes! All products imported through BeautyFlow are fully compliant with Saudi Food and Drug Authority (SFDA) regulations. We handle all necessary documentation and ensure your products meet local requirements.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, STCpay), Apple Pay, and local payment options. All transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption.

Shipping times vary based on your location within Saudi Arabia. Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days after the shipping group is complete. Express shipping options are available for faster delivery.
